Chicken Katsu

Japanese style fried chicken

Can also be used to make Tonkatsu, just use pork cutlets instead of chicken. Serve with white rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to 1/2 inch thickness
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 Tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup of pank bread crumbs
  • 1 cup of oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepeer. Place the flour, egg and panko crumbs into separate shallow dishes. Coat the chicken breasts in flour, shacking off any excess. Dip them into the egg, and then press into the panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
  2. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken in the hot oil, and cook 3 or 4 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
